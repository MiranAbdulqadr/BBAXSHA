About BBAXSHA

BBAXSHA is more than just a charity organization – we're a bridge between abundance and need. Our mission is simple yet powerful: collecting unwanted clothing from generous donors and ensuring they reach those who need them most.

Every piece of clothing tells a story, and through BBAXSHA, we give these stories new chapters. We believe that dignity comes not just from having clothes to wear, but from knowing that someone cares enough to share.

Join us in creating a world where no one has to go without the basic dignity of proper clothing.

How We Help

Clothing Collection

We collect unwanted clothes from your home or office. Schedule a pickup and we'll handle the rest with care and respect.

✨

Quality Sorting

Every donated item is carefully inspected, cleaned, and sorted to ensure only the best reaches those in need.

❤️

Community Distribution

We work directly with local communities to identify families and individuals who would benefit most from donations.
